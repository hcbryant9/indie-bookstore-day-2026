Participating Shops

Bookstores Around Richmond

Abi's Books & Brews

Mon-Thu: 5am-7pm
Fri-Sat: 5am-9pm

1212 1/2 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23220

A relaxed and cozy coffee shop and bookstore, Abi's serves warm drinks, quick bites, and comfy reads.

bbgb

Mon–Sat: 10am–6pm
Sun: 12am–5pm

3003 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23221

Located in Carytown, bbgb is a warm and well-curated children's specialty bookstore with new books for kids of all ages.

Brian's Books

Fri-Sun: 11am-6pm

2514 P St, Richmond, VA 23223

The newest brick-and-mortar bookshop in Richmond, Brian's specializes in used and vintage books. Long forgotten and well-loved books, magazines, comics, and a curated zine selection can be found here.

Book People

Mon–Sat: 10am–7pm

10464 Ridgefield Pkwy, Henrico, VA 23233

A general new and used bookshop, Book People has been a beloved neighborhood book stop since 1980.

Fountain Bookstore

Tue–Fri: 11am-6pm
Sat: 10am-5pm
Sun: 10am-4pm

1307 E Cary St, Richmond, VA 23219

Located in the heart of Richmond, Fountain has been a new, general interest bookshop since 1978 and hosts the most author programming in the state.

ILYSM Books

Tue-Fri: 11am-5pm
Sat: 10am-5pm
Sun: 10am-3pm

119 W Brookland Park Blvd, Richmond, VA 23222

A Filipino, LGBTQ, woman-owned bookstore in the Brookland Park neighborhood, ILYSM is a used store with a focus on hard-to-find titles and other forms of media.

Little Bookshop

Tue-Thu: 11am-5pm
Fri-Sat: 11am-4pm

1244 Sycamore Square Dr, Midlothian, VA 23113

A small and welcoming storefront, The Little Bookshop has books for readers of all ages.

Midlothian Book Exchange

Tue-Sat: 10am-5pm

13198 Midlothian Tpke, Midlothian, VA 23113

A massive used bookstore, Midlothian Book Exchange's mission is to make a wide range of books accessible to readers of all ages.

Shelf Life

Mon-Sat: 10am-6pm
Sun: 11am-6pm

2913 W Cary St, Richmond, VA 23221

The largest indie bookshop in the city, Shelf Life has a lower level filled with new books, and an upstairs that houses multiple rooms of used books across all genres.

Small Friend Record and Books

Wed-Fri: 11am-6pm
Sat-Sun: 11am-5pm

1 N Lombardy St, Richmond, VA 23220

Well curated and eclectic, Small Friend offers new and used books, along with zines, records, cassettes, and more.

Resist Booksellers

Mon-Sat: 11am-6pm
Sun: 11am-5pm

233 N Sycamore St Suite B, Petersburg, VA 23803

A community-centric bookstore in Petersburg, Resist supports and uplifts dreamers, builders, activists, and the books they love.
